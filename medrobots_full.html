    UltraBot

    Introducing the UltraBot, the ultimate UV-C light disinfection robot engineered for healthcare environments.

    Order Now

    Safety

    Since UV-C light is harmful to humans, it automatically stops UV-C radiation as soon as it detects a person within 3 meters.

    Autonomous Navigation

    With high precision positioning and mapping, operates autonomously through complex environments without requiring human intervention.

    Critical Zone Coverage

    Identifies handrails and door knobs accurately and ensures consistent and repeated disinfection across these highly touched areas.

    Set and Forget

    Once the battery needs recharging, it automatically goes to the charging dock and goes back to work once is charged.
